Megan Talley practices public relations, specializing in media and community relations,
thought leadership communications, event planning, social media and public relations
writing. She graduated from Louisiana State University's Manship School of Mass
Communication in 2014 with a bachelor's degree in mass communication and minors in
business administration and leadership development. As communications specialist for
NRG, a Fortune 500 company, Megan supports external communications for the company's
retail electricity brands Reliant and Green Mountain Energy.
